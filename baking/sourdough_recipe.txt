Sourdough Bread Recipe

Ingredients: 
- 500g bread flour 
- 350g water (warm, about 80°F / 27°C) 
- 100g active sourdough starter (100% hydration) 
- 10g fine sea salt

Instructions: 
1. Mix the starter, water, and flour until no dry bits
remain. Cover and rest for 1 hour (autolyse). 

2. Add salt and knead
gently until incorporated. 

3. Bulk ferment for 4–5 hours at room
temperature, performing stretch-and-folds every 30 minutes for the first
2 hours. 

4. Shape the dough into a round and rest for 20 minutes. 

5.Final shape into boule or batard, place in a floured banneton, and
refrigerate overnight. 

6. Preheat oven and Dutch oven to 475°F (245°C).
Bake covered for 20 minutes, then uncovered for 25–30 minutes until
golden brown. 

7. Cool completely before slicing.

Tips: - Use filtered water for best fermentation. - Adjust proofing time
based on temperature (warmer = faster).
